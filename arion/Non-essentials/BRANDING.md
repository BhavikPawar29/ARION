### Design Principles

1. **Glassmorphism**
   - Frosted glass effects
   - Subtle transparency
   - Backdrop blur
   - Soft shadows

2. **Gradients**
   - Smooth color transitions
   - Purple to violet primary
   - Risk-based color scales

3. **Depth**
   - Layered elements
   - Elevation through shadows
   - Z-axis hierarchy

4. **Animation**
   - Smooth transitions (0.3s)
   - Hover effects
   - Loading states
   - Micro-interactions
